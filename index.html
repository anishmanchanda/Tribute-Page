Here's a timeline of Mahatma Gandhi

  • 1891-Called to the bar at University College London and returns to India
  • 1893-Goes to South Africa and stays for 21 years
  • 1894-Helps finf the naitonal Indian Congress to fight discrimination in South Africa
  • 1900-Founds the Natal India Ambulance Corps and volunteers as a stretch-bearer in the Boer war
  • 1906-Employs his form of nonviolent protests for the first time in Johannesburg
  • 1915-Returns to India
  • 1917-Using nonviolent protests, Gandhi wins concessions from British landlords for Chamaran Farmers
  • 1919-After Jalianwala Bagh massacre, Gandhi demands a nonviolent approach from Indians in their protests
  • 1920-Becomes leader of the political Indian National Congress
  • 1922-Imprisoned for sedition, serves 2 years
  • 1930-Indian Natinal Congress declares the Independence of India
  • 1942Call for the British to 'Quit India' in a speech in Mumbai - arrested an imprisoned for almost 2 years
  • 1947-India officialy becomes an independent country
  • 1948-Gandhi is assisinated by Nathuram Godse, a Hindu nationalist
